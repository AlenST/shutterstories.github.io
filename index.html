About Me [ALEN S THOMAS]

I'm a Computer Science Engineering student at Dayananda Sagar University. The very first thought that goes into my mind when I see something beautiful is how could I let others have a glimpse of what I witness. Photography has turned from a hobby to more of a passion and a way to express how I perceive the world around me. My biggest inspiration will always be Mr.Auditya Venkatesh how's more famously known as AudiPhotography. So, I give to you my page, Shutter Stories.
